Artist

Momo Eloua

Berlin × Morocco. Street pressure, nightlife energy, and pride.

Momo Eloua is a Moroccan rapper based in Berlin. His sound blends raw street attitude with the hypnotic atmosphere of late-night club culture.

His music reflects the tension between struggle and ambition — loyalty, dominance, celebration, and the beauty that can grow out of difficult circumstances.
